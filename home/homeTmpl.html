What is Blue Wavers?

We love music!

That's a pretty bold and broad statement, and frankly, is false! We love a very specific form of music. No, it's not that dramatic bump of the kick drum, nor that 'flawless' vocal performance on television, or even that 'dope' guy in front of the mansion with all of the ladies in his hot tub. We cringe at the messages the media is secretly portraying to this generation. This is where Indie Rock shines.

We write music!

There's something to be said about the power of music, and the effect that it has on people and their minds. Little do we realize it, but every word spoken to us will directly connect to the decisions we make. That being said, the type of music you listen to correlates to the type of person you will become. It's all kind of cliche, but take a step back and look at yourself; look at your past. When have you felt your truest bliss, when have you seen yourself at your best, and when were your darkest times? What types of music were you listening to in those moments? Do you see the connection?

We support music!

In the end, it's all up to the world to decide its own fate. Every choice we make sets a path, and we must walk down that path no matter the consequences. Whether it be guilt, pain, pride, pleasure, it always comes, even if we don't see it. These things we do, these things we say in our hazy mindset; These... are our Blind Intentions.

Why 'Blue Wavers'?

Ah, deciding on a band name.. The equivalent of staying up all night before a big event in the morning. In the moment it's awesome, but you always regret it in the end.

We were always into the 80's stuff. Taylor practically lived in the decade, but thats a whole other topic. In the 80's, 'new wave' was a hit genre of music. It pretty much set up what we know now as alternative music. Catchy hooks, unforgettable lyrics, and the introduction of popular synthetic instruments became the voice of the decade, and who still doesn't love the older stuff!
